About Him

Singer And Dancer

Jeon Jungkook (Korean: 전정국) is a Grammy Award-nominated singer, songwriter, producer, and dancer.Jungkook is the main vocalist, center, lead dancer, and sub rapper of the band, BTS. Jungkook is known for having absolute pitch (or perfect pitch), a rare ability that approximately only one in ten thousand people have. He is the youngest member and the designated “Face of the Group”, due to his massive popularity. Jungkook is also a director, filmmaker, choreographer, painter, athlete, and musician. Often referred to as the “Ace of K-Pop” by the media, Jungkook is recognized as the most talented, popular, and successful K-Pop Idol in history.
